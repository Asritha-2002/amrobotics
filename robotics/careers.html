Careers at Am Robotics

If you're looking for a career opportunity with a growing company in a growing industry, you've landed in the right place. The robotics industry is one of the most dynamic and evolving industries in the world. Whether you're a first-time jobseeker or an experienced professional, we’re happy you landed here and hope that you’ll discover an opportunity that excites you.

Am Robotics is one of the leading organizations in supplying and developing Mechanical parts, modules, DIY kits, Product Design, Manufacturing, and many more. Every organization has different work portfolios, and every employee contributes to the development of the organization. We have different departments catering to Sales, Customer Support, Logistics, and Procurement, and all departments work and interlink with each other.

Since we are a growing company, we are joining hands with good professionals in our team. To improve you, we follow the KPR (Key Performance Results) methodology, and responsibilities are delegated accordingly to employees for their better growth.

Feeling excited to see yourself at a different position in the coming years with the dreams of Am Robotics? Then join us and make it happen! Work with our young & energetic team. Progression is based solely on merit & performance. No orthodox or rigid rules will limit your growth.
